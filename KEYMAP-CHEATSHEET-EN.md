### Function Menu (LP-GREEN) - BETA 06
- **Set Trigger** - Toggle trigger at level 6
- **Set Ignore (SP #)** - Exclude frequency
- **Freq ‚Üí VFO (PTT)** - Copy freq to VFO
- **Clear Ignores** - Reset exclusions
